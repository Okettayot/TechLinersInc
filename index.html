About Us

A team of dedicated innovators set out to tackle critical global challenges, harnessing the potential of artificial intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT), and machine learning. With an unwavering commitment to sustainability and data-driven solutions, we have consistently strived to optimize farming practices, ensure food security, and minimize waste. Our journey is fueled by the determination to create a more sustainable and efficient future for agriculture, making a positive impact on communities worldwide.

Sparky briquettes

At Sparky, our commitment to sustainability and environmental consciousness has led us to introduce our premium Sparky Briquettes. These briquettes are meticulously crafted from a combination of our top-tier dryer and spice manufacturing waste, as well as kitchen waste, providing an eco-friendly, clean, and highly efficient alternative to traditional charcoal. Our unique formulation, enriched with honey wax, ensures a longer burning duration, delivering a hot blue flame that enables quicker cooking and heating. With Sparky Briquettes, you can embrace a cleaner, more sustainable energy source that minimizes waste and encourages more efficient energy consumption.

Sparky Dryer
The Sparky Dryer is a pioneering agricultural technology that enables farmers and households to effectively reduce food waste and extend the shelf life of their produce. This innovative dryer leverages solar energy to efficiently dehydrate fruits, vegetables, and other agricultural products, preserving their nutritional value and quality. With its user-friendly design and sustainable operation, the Sparky Dryer offers a practical solution to post-harvest challenges, allowing farmers to enhance their income and contribute to a more sustainable food ecosystem. Experience the power of the Sparky Dryer in revolutionizing the way you preserve your agricultural produce
The Sparky Dryer combines the use of clean biofuel, specifically briquettes derived from waste, with the energy harnessed from solar power. This energy is stored in a battery and used to facilitate the drying of food items within approximately 5 hours, all while maintaining a controlled temperature automatically. The 15kg dryer is equipped with a temperature control system, a biofuel stove, a 15W solar panel, a 10ah battery, a charge controller, and a 10-meter cable.
